01 — About

The Story So Far

I'm Venu Gopal Nukathoti, a passionate frontend developer who thrives at the intersection of design and technology. I build responsive, visually compelling web experiences using modern HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and Bootstrap.

My journey into tech started with curiosity — asking "how does this work?" about every website I visited. That curiosity evolved into a practice of learning by building, iterating, and pushing the limits of what's possible in the browser.

I believe great software is invisible: it simply works, it delights, and it gets out of the way. That philosophy drives every line I write.
